Associate Professor

Yanggang Feng

Associate Professor of Mechanical Engineering and Automation
Beihang University

Yanggang Feng is an Associate Professor in the School of Mechanical Engineering and Automation at Beihang University. His research focuses on robotics, specifically wearable robots, rehabilitation robots (with a focus on knee rehabilitation), exoskeletons, and embodied AI. He is particularly interested in the design and control of electro-hydraulic servo and damping systems for robotic applications.

Dr. Feng received his Ph.D. degree from Peking University. Before joining Beihang University, he conducted research at The University of Tokyo. He has published works in high-impact journals such as Nature Communications and IEEE Transactions on Robotics. His recent work includes the development of a power-free knee rehabilitation robot for home-based isokinetic training.

Dr. Feng teaches courses on robotics and control systems, and supervises graduate and undergraduate students in the areas of robotics and embodied AI.
